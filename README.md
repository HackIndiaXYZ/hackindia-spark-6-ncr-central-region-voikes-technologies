## 🧠 Problem Statement

In today’s digital era, most learning platforms remain passive and fail to actively engage users, leading to decreased motivation and poor knowledge retention. Learners often struggle with monotonous content delivery, lack of personalization, and absence of interactive elements that make learning enjoyable and effective. As attention spans decrease and distractions increase, there is a growing need for a learning system that makes education more engaging, adaptive, and user-centric.

### 🔹 Key Problems

- Low engagement due to static and non-interactive content  
- Lack of personalized learning experiences  
- Decreasing attention span of learners  
- Absence of motivation and reward mechanisms  
- Limited real-time feedback and interaction  
- Learning feels monotonous rather than enjoyable
